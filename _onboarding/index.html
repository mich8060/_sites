Day One

The first half of day one will include a training session from Human Resources. At noon the design team will pick you up for lunch (provided by Backcountry). This will be an excellent opportunity for us to get to know each other better and start us off on the right foot.

After lunch we will show you where you will be sitting and get your going on some general setup items you are going to want to take care of. Before you arrived, a Human Resource representative should have emailed you a link with information for you to fill out such as 401k, W-2, etc. If you have not received and/or completed this work, this will be a great time for you to get started on that information. We recommend you use some of your time on the first day to review this books and get your computer organized.

You will receive a series of invitations to the training sessions mentioned in the training section of this document as well as to your weekly recurring meetings. These recurring meetings include daily stand-ups, weekly grooming sessions, team design reviews, weekly manager 1:1, etc. If you have any questions around these meetings please feel free to reach out to either your product manager or your design manager.

Week One

We hope to have most of the trainings in the training section completed within your first week. Understandably this might come as a bit of an information overload, but we are always available to answer your questions.

In your first few days both your Manager & Product Manager will sit down with you and introduce you to the first project you will be working on here at Backcountry. As you ramp up on this project please feel free to use any of the designers or managers as resources to help you better understand any questions you might have about the project.

File Storage Continued...

Google Drive
When IT gets you setup with a username & password, this also includes a company paid account for google drive. You can access this drive via the following url:

https://drive.google.com/drive/u/0/my-drive

Use your backcountry provided credentials to login to your account. This drive include up to 30 GB of space that you can use to store important information or information that needs to be shared with other individuals within the company.

Note: By default all documents will be set to private, but you can allow access to your information by:
  1. Tapping Control and clicking on any item.
  2. Selecting “Share” from the dropdown of options.
  3. From here you can share your document in one of two ways:
    1. Enter the email address of the person you want to share your document with.
    2. Clicking advanced and changing the permissions of the document so that anyone can see it. WIth this option you will still need to send a link for your document to the individuals you wish to share it with.
