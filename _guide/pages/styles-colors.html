Colors

Specific colors are outlined for use across Backcountry.com. Designers should not introduce new colors to our pallet without a design review.

Primary Colors
Colors Families
Notes:
  1. Black: Main branding color used most often.
  2. Blue: Used in some CTA's
  3. Green: Used in the community to denote following. Used as a secondary color on Explore pages.
  4. Orange: Used to denote a sale or discount pricing.
  5. Gray: Used to achieve hierarchy with body copy. Choose between gray and light gray.
  6. Light Gray: Used to achieve hierarchy and for sub-text.Choose between light gray and gray.
  7. Lighter Gray: Used for dropdown borders/arrows. Choose between lighter gray and lightest gray.
  8. Lightest Gray: Used for background colors. Choose between lighter gray and lightest gray.
  9. Red: Used to callout outlet items and error messaging.
