Product Listing Item

The Product Listing Item (PLI) is a summary of an available product and direct link to the product description page for the displayed item. Pertinent product information (e.g. brand and product name, price, and community rating) is surfaced in close proximity to the product image to ensure confidence when navigating to learn more about the desired product.
