Buttons

Buttons are a key form of navigation within our UI. Our button patterns are designed to provide simple yet clear communication to the user on desired next steps in the flow, and to provide clear hierarchy when multiple calls to action exist. To achieve these goals there are 4 main button types available for use.

Prominent Buttons

The solid blue button is a primary button that signifies important actions and seeks to give emphasis to the mainly colorless interface. These should be used sparingly and only for primary “next steps” within the funnel (such as "Add to Cart") and should never appear more than once on a page (with the exception of the cart).
